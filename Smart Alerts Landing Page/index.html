Frequently Asked Questions

📍 Is Smart Alerts available in all cities?

No, Smart Alerts is currently focused on Tier 1, Tier 2, and select Tier 3 cities in India. We're expanding rapidly!

🛒 Can I make purchases through the app?

Smart Alerts is designed to help you find the best local deals. You’ll need to visit the store to complete your purchase.

📲 Is there any cost to use Smart Alerts?

Nope! Smart Alerts is completely free for customers. Retailers can choose from free and premium listing plans.

📤 How can I list my store?

Simply click on "List Your Store" above or contact us. We'll guide you through the quick registration process.

🔔 How often are offers updated?

Offers are updated in real-time as retailers post new promotions. You’ll be instantly notified when something new is nearby.

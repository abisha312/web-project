Smart Alerts: Get the Best Local Deals!

Overview

Smart Alerts is a mobile app designed to help users discover the best deals from local stores. Users can browse discounts on various products, compare offers, and make informed shopping decisions while supporting local businesses.

Key Features

  • Dashboard 📊 - Displays real-time best deals from local markets and businesses.
  • Category Listings 📂 - Products divided into Food, Accessories, FMCG, and Clothing.
  • Subcategories 🍎👗 - E.g., Food → Vegetarian, Non-Veg, Fruits, etc.
  • Search & Compare 🔎 - Find specific items and compare the best local offers.
  • Real-Time Notifications 📢 - Get alerts for new deals and limited-time discounts.
  • Offline Purchase Support 🛍️ - No online delivery; only in-store purchases to boost foot traffic.
